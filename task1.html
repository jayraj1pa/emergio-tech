"Assassin's Creed Valhalla" is an action role-playing video game developed by Ubisoft. Released in 2020, it is the twelfth major installment in the popular Assassin's Creed series. Set in the Viking Age, the game follows Eivor, a Viking raider who becomes embroiled in the conflict between the Brotherhood of Assassins and the Templar Order. Players embark on a grand adventure, exploring the stunning landscapes of Norway and England, participating in epic battles, and building their own settlement. With a compelling narrative, rich historical detail, and a seamless blend of stealth and combat gameplay, Assassin's Creed Valhalla offers an immersive experience that delves into the complex world of Norse mythology and Viking history. The game received acclaim for its engaging storyline, expansive open-world design, and the ability for players to shape their own Viking saga through choices and decisions made throughout the game.
